+ 52 lower and upper case characters
+ 32 special characters

94\*94\*94\*94\*94\*94\*94\*94 = 6,095,689,385,000,000 combinations

450K\*450K\*450K\*450K = 41,006,250,000,000,000,000,000 combinations
